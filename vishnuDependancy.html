About Green Belt


Natural Habitats

Green belt areas often include forest,wet lands,green lands and other natural habitants that provide habitats for wildlife and help maintain biodiversity.

Agriculture Land

Many green belt areas are used for farming,providing food and resources for near communities.

Parks and Recreational areas

Some green belt areas are designated as parts are recreeational areas ,providing oppurtunities for hiking,liking,pinching and other out door activities.

Water Resourses

Many green belt areas are designated as parts contain rivers,lakes and other bodies of water that are important for both ecological and recreation purpouse.
